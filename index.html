Simon Kohaut

PhD Candidate · AI & ML Lab · TU Darmstadt

I am a PhD candidate at the Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Lab at TU Darmstadt, co-supervised by Kristian Kersting and Devendra Singh Dhami.

My research sits at the intersection of neuro-symbolic AI and responsible reasoning systems, developing methods that combine the representational powers of neural learning with the interpretability and reasoning capabilities of symbolic systems. I am particularly interested in deploying such systems in safety-critical domains, including robotics and autonomous transportation, where reliability, explainability, and formal guarantees are essential.

I care about building AI that is not only performant, but also verifiable, transparent, and safe by design, contributing to a future where intelligent systems can be responsibly integrated into the physical world.
