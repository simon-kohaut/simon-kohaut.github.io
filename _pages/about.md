I am a PhD researcher at the [Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Lab](https://ml-research.github.io) at [TU Darmstadt](https://www.tu-darmstadt.de), supervised by [Prof. Kristian Kersting](https://ml-research.github.io/people/kkersting/) and co-supervised by the [Honda Research Institute Europe](https://www.honda-ri.de/).

My research sits at the intersection of **neuro-symbolic AI** and **trustworthy systems** — developing methods that combine the statistical power of neural learning with the interpretability and reasoning capabilities of symbolic systems. I am particularly interested in deploying such systems in safety-critical domains, including **robotics** and **autonomous transportation**, where reliability, explainability, and formal guarantees are essential.

More broadly, I care about building AI that is not only performant, but also **verifiable**, **transparent**, and **safe by design** — contributing to a future where intelligent systems can be responsibly integrated into the physical world.
